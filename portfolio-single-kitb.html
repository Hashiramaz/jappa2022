Project Description

Kids in a Tub Boat was an in-house game I worked on together with Tiny Hydra. The game is about two kids exploring a world aboard their bathtub-shaped ship. With lots of humorous elements and a really cute aesthetic, it's definitely one of the coolest projects I've worked on.



In this project I was challenged to discover new technologies, worked with Unreal Engine and made some asset pipelines in Houdini. The game had some very complex and interesting challenges to work with, such as water simulation and application of toon shaders in unreal.

Project Date 2021
Role Engineer, Tech Artist
